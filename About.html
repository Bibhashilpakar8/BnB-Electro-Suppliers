About us

BnB Electro Suppliers has committed itself to fulfilling the needs of a diverse customer base in Nepal by providing affordable and high-quality lights. As time passed, our selection of products has broadened to encompass various light types, ranging from small bulbs to grand chandeliers. We offer all kinds of lights, switches and other electric appliances that make your house, office and hospitalities brighter and decorative. BnB Electro Suppliers focuses on providing energy-efficient as well as cost-efficient solutions to their customers. We also offer installation services along with rental services.
